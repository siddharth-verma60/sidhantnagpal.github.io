We all know about Mozilla, through the awesome web-browser Firefox.
But actually, Mozilla is much more than the web-browser. (Click here to know about Firefox OS)

Mozilla is a non-profit-organization, fighting for a mission to promote openness, innovation & opportunity on the Web we all love!

Mozilla is training people like us, to be a generation of Webmakers.

How Can You Contribute?

  • By just using Firefox! (or any other Mozilla projectlike Thunderbird)
  • Facing any problem with Firefox? Report a Bug, and you have done a great job for Mozilla.
  • Spread the word for Open Web and Mozilla, become an affiliate.

  • Help other users in your free time, you just need a twitter account for that! :)
  • Contribute in the code! Mozilla is OpenSource!
  • Use Firefox Aurora, shape the future of Firefox.

Find a summer code party near you, And start learning about Web, and how to improve & contribute to it!
